Product Variants
Product 01 — WW/CW
Tunable White · 2700K – 6500K
A precision white-light bulb capable of smoothly transitioning the colour temperature across the full warm-to-cool spectrum. Two independent LED channels (Warm White + Cool White) are driven by separate MOSFET stages, enabling continuous CCT blending.
  • Independent PWM control per WW and CW channel
  • IRF530N N-channel MOSFET per channel — low-side switching
  • IR2112 gate driver for reliable high-current MOSFET actuation
  • 12V power supply — SMD LEDs on heatsinks for thermal management
Product 02 — WW + RGB
Full Colour · Millions of Colors · CRI > 80
Advanced variant combining RGB LEDs with a dedicated Warm White channel. RGB mixing is tuned to reach high-quality cool-white output — ensuring CRI above 80 even in full-colour mode, preserving colour rendering accuracy alongside creative lighting.
  • 4 independent MOSFET channels: Red, Green, Blue, Warm White
  • RGB blending algorithm targeting CRI > 80 at white output
  • 6× 3W RGB SMD LEDs + 6× 1W WW SMD LEDs
  • AI-assisted colour pattern detection & adaptation
💡 Direct Relevance to Schréder: This project demonstrates hands-on understanding of LED driver electronics, multi-channel MOSFET switching, colour temperature management, and smart control integration — the exact technical domain of Schréder's intelligent outdoor and architectural lighting products.

Hardware Architecture
CONTROL
ESP32
Prototype MCU · Wi-Fi + BT
Main controller for prototype development. Generates multi-channel PWM signals for LED dimming and communicates with mobile app over Bluetooth. Production target: BK7231 (cost-optimised SoC).
GATE DRIVER
IR2112
Half-Bridge MOSFET Driver
Provides proper gate drive voltage and timing for the IRF530N MOSFETs. Ensures clean switching and protects the ESP32 GPIO pins from high-current LED loads.
SWITCHING
IRF530N
N-Channel Power MOSFET
High-current low-side switch per LED channel. One MOSFET per channel enables independent PWM dimming of WW, CW, R, G, B — up to 4 channels in the RGB variant.
EMITTERS — WW/CW
SMD LEDs (1W)
WW + CW · Per-Channel Heatsinks
High-power 1W SMD emitters in warm white and cool white, mounted on individual heatsinks for thermal management at full drive current.
EMITTERS — RGB
SMD RGB LEDs (3W)
Full-Spectrum · High-Power
3W RGB emitters enabling millions of colour combinations. Combined with the WW channel, RGB mixing is tuned to reach CRI > 80 at white output.
POWER
12V Power Supply
Regulated DC
12V regulated supply powers both the LED array and MOSFET driver circuit. ESP32 powered via onboard 3.3V regulator from the same rail.

Key Outcomes
  • ◆ Designed and built two distinct LED driver hardware variants (WW/CW and WW+RGB), each with independent multi-channel MOSFET switching — demonstrating practical power electronics and LED driver design capability.
  • ◆ Achieved CRI > 80 using RGB + WW mixing strategy — going beyond simple colour output to ensure professionally-acceptable colour rendering, aligned with industry-standard lighting quality metrics.
  • ◆ Defined a production-ready roadmap — identifying the BK7231 as a cost-optimised MCU replacement for the ESP32 prototype, and planning PCB consolidation and enclosure design for market viability.
  • ◆ Implemented AI-assisted user pattern detection for adaptive scheduling — matching the direction of Schréder's intelligent outdoor lighting control systems that adapt to real-world usage patterns.
  • ◆ Developed wellness-driven lighting modes (Wake-Up, Focus, Reading, Evening) — demonstrating understanding of human-centric lighting design, a key value proposition in premium smart lighting products.
  • ◆ Executed full self-directed product development lifecycle — from concept and component selection, through BOM costing, prototype assembly, firmware development, and production migration planning — with no institutional structure or deadline.
